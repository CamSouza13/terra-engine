Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how [Terra Labs, Inc.] ("Terra", "we") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Terra platform (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — your email, workspace name, and hashed password. We never store passwords in plain text.
  • Operational data — sensor readings, estimates, configuration, alert rules, and node metadata you or your nodes submit to the Service.
  • Support and orders — the email address and details you provide when contacting support or requesting hardware.
  • Usage and logs — an audit log of actions in your workspace, and standard technical logs (e.g. IP address, timestamps) used for security and reliability.
  • Payment data — handled by our payment processor; we do not store full card numbers.

2. How we use information

  • To provide, operate, secure, and improve the Service.
  • To run inference, alerts, and control features on the data you submit.
  • To communicate with you — account, transactional, support, and (where permitted) product messages.
  • To process orders and payments and to comply with legal obligations.

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate the Service (for example, hosting, email delivery, and payment processing), under confidentiality obligations; when required by law; or in connection with a business transfer. Your operational data is scoped to your workspace and is not shared with other customers.

4. Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion as described below.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including salted password hashing, scoped credentials, and encrypted transport. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these, email us at the address below. You may also unsubscribe from non-essential emails at any time.

7. Data controllers and your customers

Where you submit data about your own operations or personnel, you are the controller of that data and are responsible for having a lawful basis to submit it; we act as a processor on your behalf.

8. International users

We operate in the United States; by using the Service you understand your information may be processed there.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

10. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be posted here with a revised date.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: hello@terralaboratories.com.
