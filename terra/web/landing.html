How it works

Three steps to a loop that watches itself.

01

Enroll a node

Mint a token in the console and run one command. The node authenticates itself and starts reporting.

A Bayesian filter fuses your readings into an estimate of what's really happening — with calibrated uncertainty and a forecast.

Get notified before a limit is crossed. Enable autonomy and Terra can act — on the edge, even offline.
